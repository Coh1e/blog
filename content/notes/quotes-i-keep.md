> The way to write a book is to actually write a book.
> —— Anne Lamott

> I would have written you a shorter letter, but I did not have the time.
> —— Pascal

> 软件是手段，不是爱好。
> —— 大概是某人

> Premature optimization is the root of all evil.
> —— Knuth

> The cost of code is in maintaining it, not writing it.
> —— 各种来源

> In a world where everything is amplified, the most subversive thing is to slow down.
> —— 我自己想到的，但显然不是

> 完成比完美好。
> —— Facebook 的内部 motto，他们后来也没做对，但这一条是对的

为什么留着：每一条都是对**自我欺骗**的纠正。我每过几个月会陷入某种自我欺骗，看到对应的引文就出来了。

详见 [[posts/the-case-against-perfect|反对完美]] 和 [[posts/the-discipline-of-finishing|完成的纪律]]。
