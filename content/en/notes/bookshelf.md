A snapshot of what's on the small shelf above my desk. Updated roughly quarterly. These are the books currently doing work — not the ones I keep for sentiment.

- Christopher Alexander — _The Nature of Order, Vol. 1_ (currently re-reading)
- John Berger — _Ways of Seeing_
- William Hazlitt — _Selected Essays_
- Ursula K. Le Guin — _Steering the Craft_
- Gwern Branwen — printout of selected long-form essays
- Yi-Fu Tuan — _Space and Place_
- Kim Stanley Robinson — _The Years of Rice and Salt_
- Annie Dillard — _The Writing Life_

Eight books. The full library is much larger; the working shelf is small on purpose. See [[en/posts/library-of-the-living]] for the rule.

I don't recommend this list for anyone else. They're the books that are doing work for me right now. Yours will be different.
